How does it even Work?

Pick Your Target Audience

First of all, you need to pick the specific audience you want to target, just like on Google Ads. This could be the location, gender, age group, income bracket. We are constantly collecting data to see what a pair of eyes cannot see and add more granularity to this demographic classification. Then you upload the ad you want to be shown on the street.

Pannom Automatically Places the Ads

Using our sensors on the billboards, we detect if the people you want to target walk by. If so, we show your ads at the optimum time for the pedestrians to pay attention.

Measure Real-time Metrics

Finally, using the same sensors, we check if pedestirans actually look at th ads, for how long and how many times. You can view this results on your dashboard and optimize your campaign accordingly.
