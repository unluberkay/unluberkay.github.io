Meet Pannom

Pannom is a platform that connects your product with people who need it most, and helps you out by optimizing your outdoor advertising budget.

Target Audiences

Pannom is your personal pair of eyes — and she’s very talented at making good guesses about the people she sees… For example you can pick: affluent, 18-25 years old females.

Buy Ads

We utilize a real-time bidding that allows you to buy ads directly through our platform — no need for an intermediary or a markup!

Monitor Performance

Using her cutting-edge hardware and software, Pannom is able to tell you exactly which demographics are seeing your ad, and when. All of this can be monitored in real-time on our website.

How does it even Work?

Pick Your Target Audience

First of all, you tell Pannom what sorts of people you most want to see your ad. This could be location, gender, age groups, or income bracket. Pannom spends a lot of time studying and learning, always getting better at connecting your product with the people who need it most.

Pannom Automatically Places the Ads

After you upload an ad through the website, Pannom will show your ad at optimal points based on who’s around to see it.

Measure Real-time Metrics

Pannom can offer you insights that old outdoor advertising just couldn’t. How many people are seeing the ad? Where? What sorts of people? How long were they looking? Pannom keeps you updated in real-time on your dashboard.
